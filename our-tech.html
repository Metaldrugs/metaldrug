Convergence Architecture

KT-Biotech unifies disciplines: molecular chemistry, systems biology, AI, and information theory.

  • SEI (Synthetic Evolving Intelligence) interprets biology and generates hypotheses in real time.

SEI — Synthetic Evolving Intelligence

Not just AI. SEI evolves.

MDC — The Molecular Language

What if molecules could compute?

  • Each MDC is a molecular program readable by SEI
