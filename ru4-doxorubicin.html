Ru⁴⁺-Doxorubicin

Oncology-Focused Metal-Drug Complex (MDC)

Overview

Ru⁴⁺-Doxorubicin is a targeted MDC designed for solid tumor environments. Ruthenium (IV) allows for pH-responsive drug release, enabling the complex to remain stable in blood but activate in acidic microenvironments such as those found in tumors.

Mechanism of Action

  • Ruthenium ion undergoes redox-based activation in low-pH conditions
  • Doxorubicin intercalates DNA and inhibits topoisomerase II once released
  • Minimal release in healthy tissues reduces off-target cytotoxicity

Key Properties

  • pH-triggered release below pH 6.8
  • Reduced cardiotoxicity profile
  • Longer circulation half-life

Application

Therapeutic Class: Cancer

Indicated for breast, ovarian, and colorectal cancers with acidic tumor cores.
