Moleculogic

An advanced molecular reasoning engine developed by Kunfirm Technologies (KT) for AI-assisted compound design, logic-based simulation, and secure therapeutic modeling.

What is Moleculogic?

Moleculogic is a logic-first AI framework for molecular design and validation. Rooted in Rational Constructivism and coordinated with MDC protocols, it provides dynamic compound synthesis suggestions, predictive toxicology, and encrypted twin creation for regulatory alignment.

Key Capabilities

  • AI-Guided Molecular Assembly (MGMA Protocols)
  • Integration with MDC discovery and tuning modules
  • Multi-tiered reasoning: from ligand scanning to redox modeling
  • Metabolic logic path prediction and interaction warning
  • Encrypted digital twin generation for pharma traceability

How to Use

  1. Access the Moleculogic dashboard (via KT Secure Partner Portal)
  2. Verify tier-level access using your organization credentials
  3. Submit a drug scaffold, coordination hypothesis, or design goal
  4. Receive validated suggestions and logic trees for synthesis
  5. Export output or proceed to MDC complexation
